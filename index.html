Who are DiabeCare ?

Welcome to Diabe Care , a passionate community of researchers, and technology enthusiasts dedicated to transforming the management of diabetes. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for diabetic patients through innovative, data-driven solutions and personalized healthcare strategies. We are focusing on areas such as blood glucose monitoring and medication tracking, AI-powered food item identification and calorie estimation, AI driven solution foot risks and activity recommendations, we aim to bridge the gap between medical science and advanced technology. Whether through collaborative research, cutting-edge machine learning applications, or patient-centered design, our goal is to revolutionize diabetic care and empower individuals to take control of their health. Join us in building a healthier future and making impactful strides in the field of diabetes management.

Our Domain

 Diabetes management has evolved with advancements in predictive analytics, real-time monitoring, and digital healthcare tools. Traditional methods like manual record-keeping and periodic check-ups often fall short in providing continuous care, increasing the risk of complications such as diabetic foot issues. DiabeCare is a smart, centralized mobile application designed to overcome these limitations by offering real-time insights, automated alerts, and personalized recommendations. It supports users by tracking blood glucose levels, monitoring physical activity, and assessing foot complication risks using machine learning. Additionally, it features AI-powered food recognition and calorie estimation to provide tailored dietary advice, while also monitoring vital health metrics such as blood pressure, heart rate, and BMI. DiabeCare presents a holistic and innovative approach to managing Type 2 diabetes and prediabetes, promoting early intervention and improved patient outcomes.

 While numerous mobile health applications support individual components of diabetes management—such as blood glucose tracking, dietary logging, or physical activity monitoring—most operate in isolation, lacking seamless integration of these critical functions. Furthermore, existing solutions often fail to incorporate real-time decision-making and machine learning models for personalized care adjustments. There is a pressing need for a unified, AI-powered system that holistically integrates dietary analysis, glucose level prediction, physical activity tracking, and diabetic complication risk assessment (e.g., foot ulcers) within a single platform. This gap limits the potential for truly personalized, preventive, and proactive diabetes management—especially in resource-constrained settings where access to continuous care is limited

 

  Food Identification & Calorie Estimation Manual food logging is error-prone and time-consuming, and most mobile apps lack accurate AI-based food recognition and calorie estimation tailored to culturally diverse diets.

  Medication Adherence Tracking Diabetic patients often miss medications due to lack of reminders, poor integration with meal and activity schedules, and absence of intelligent tracking mechanisms in existing tools.

  Physical Activity Recommendation Current activity trackers rarely provide personalized, real-time exercise recommendations based on blood glucose responses, which is essential for optimal diabetes management.

  To develop an AI-powered food recognition system using image processing(Yolo V8 Model) and calorie estimation models that can identify culturally relevant meals and provide accurate nutritional insights.

  To implement an intelligent medication tracking module that monitors dosage, timing, and interactions with meals and physical activity, and provides timely alerts to improve adherence(SVC Model).

  To create a personalized activity management system using machine learning algorithms (Random Forest) that analyzes health parameters and suggests real-time adjustments to exercise plans.

  To design and integrate a mobile-based foot ulcer detection model using deep learning (MobileNetV2) to classify foot conditions and assess risk levels for early intervention.

What do we provide?

Smart Food Recognition & Calorie Estimation Engine



Automatically identify meals from real-world images using convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and estimate calorie content based on predefined nutritional datasets. This AI-powered system helps diabetic patients make informed dietary choices by offering accurate, culturally adapted calorie insights in real time.

Classify Risk Of Hyperglycemia



Monitor and track medication intake schedules with AI-driven reminders and interaction analysis. The system detects irregularities in medication adherence and correlates dosage with blood glucose levels and meal timing to generate personalized medication alerts for better disease management.

Personalized Activity Management & Suggestion



Leverage machine learning models like Random Forest to analyze user-specific activity patterns, blood glucose data, and physiological indicators. The system dynamically recommends exercise routines, adjustments in intensity or duration, and warns against activities that may elevate health risks.

Diabetic Foot Risk Prediction System



Use maching learning techniques (e.g., MobileNetV2) to detect and categorize foot ulcers through image analysis. The model classifies severity levels and provides early alerts, helping prevent complications such as infections or amputations through timely medical intervention.
